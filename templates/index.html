Chocolate Bar Rating

This project's goal is to analyse Bars' ratings and to draw some conclusions. The dataset has information about different types of Cocoa Beans, the percentage of Cocoa, Companies of the bars and couple of other columns

Pie Chart
{%include'rating.html'%}
The most common rating for bars is 3.5 among all the countries

Now let's find out what countries are the best and the worst in terms of Ratings

Bar Chart
{%include'leading.html'%}
It is obvious that Chile is the best in terms of creating bars, which has mean rating - 3.75
Bar Chart
{%include'nonleading.html'%}
India's average rating is only 2.50, which makes it the loosing country among others
Treemap Chart
{%include'countries.html'%}
So, we get, that U.S.A. produces way more bars than other countries. Let us find out, whether it is true, that the quality is also the leading parameter for bars made in U.S.A.
Pie Chart
{%include'usa.html'%}
Despite the fact, that U.S.A. produces most bars, the ratings for its bars are not so high. In fact, the most common ones are '3.00' and '3.50', which shows us the average quality of American chocolate bars.
Sunburst Chart
{%include'companies.html'%}
So, here we can see, that the biggest producers are 'Soma', 'Bonnat', 'Fresco', 'Pralus' and 'A.Morin'.
Let's visualize these companies in a more precise way
{%include'Lcompanies.html'%}
This graph helps us to see, how company's rating has been changing during 10 years

Do any relations between Rating and Cocoa-Beans exist?

Scatter Chart
{%include'beansrating.html'%}
Only Cocoa-Beans 'Blend' and 'Trinitario' got the highest mark. We also can see, that Cocoa-Beans do not really affect on the rating of the bar, as different bars with the same Cocoa-Bean may differ in the rating a lot.
Scatter Chart
{%include'locrat.html'%}
Where the Bean originates from
{%include'beanrat.html'%}
We get, that only Venezuela and another country have perfect beans for the future bars. But it's not the fact, that if a company gets beans from there, the bar will have successful rating. Mostly, countries from North America can produce beans for the future best bars.
